About Me

I could just post my years of experience and the technologies I know but frankly that’s what LinkedIn is for. I’d rather make a little paragraph of each part of what it means to be me as a professional.

My experience in web development over the years has led me to a deep understanding on UI/UX, which strangely is my expertise even though my formation is as a Software Engineer. I believe it makes an interesting and rather rare combination. I know most developers won’t like hearing this but the truth is people label us as “square” and I’m just here to prove them wrong. If anything we are creative beings beyond everyone’s understanding.

As a developer I understand the idea of teamwork and embrace it completely. But as my personality goes I do my best work alone to a certain point to then integrate it with the group which requires a lot of self-management and the ability to keep myself motivated. I don’t believe on leaving things in the middle, I either get there or die trying.

As any person I’m not perfect and it is always my goal to grow in a positive way as a professional as well as a human being. Self-constructive criticism is one of the things I always see as completely necessary for a healthy work environment and personal growth.

Last but not least...at all, as a hardcore gamer I’ve always found a deep interest in being part of a game development company and even expand my abilities from web development to game development along the way. I’ve admire gaming companies my whole life not only as the best set of developers/designers/animators/administrative teams, because I really don’t think there is a more complex piece of art than a game, but also as the most caring and creative group of people you can be a part of, and I dream of being a part of the joy brought to millions of players from a world they could never even imagine if it wasn’t for the people behind these amazing projects. My dream is to be a part of the adventure these companies take you on.
