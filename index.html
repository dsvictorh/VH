About Me

I’m Victor Herrera, a software engineer and web developer with passion for simplistic yet amazing web solutions and the world of video games. Though I could just describe to you my experience and knowledge I honestly think that’s what my LinkedIn Profile is for, which you are more than welcome to check out. Instead I’d rather describe myself as a person and a little bit of what it means to be me as a professional.

Over the years I have found myself deeply interested about the visual concept of my projects to better understand what is it that people observe, not just see on a page. Some people have the wrong idea that as developers we are a bit “square”, but I think we are creative beings beyond the uderstanding of so many, and we can still be the logical geniuses they think we are. I believe this way of seeing yourself as a developer makes an interesting combination towards delivering only the best solutions and giving all your dedication to the projects you create.

As a gamer I’ve always found a deep interest in being part of a gaming company and even expand my abilities from web development to game development along the way. These companies, in my opinion, can only subsist because the people that are part of it are to me the most hard-working, creative and caring professionals, they love what they do and want to share only the best to the world. I dream of being a part of the joy brought to millions of players from these communities of amazing people. My dream is to be a part of the adventure these companies take you on.
