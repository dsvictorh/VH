About Me

I’m Victor Herrera, a software engineer and web developer with passion for simplistic yet amazing web solutions and the world of video game development. Though I could just describe to you my experience and knowledge I honestly think that’s what my LinkedIn Profile is for, which you are more than welcome to check out. Instead I’d rather describe myself as a person and a little bit of what it means to be me as a professional.

Over the years I have found myself deeply interested about the visual concept of my projects to better understand what is it that people observe, not just see on a page. Some people have the wrong idea that as developers we are a bit “square”, but I think we are creative beings beyond the uderstanding of so many, and we can still be the logical geniuses they think we are. I believe this way of seeing yourself as a developer makes an interesting combination towards delivering only the best solutions and giving all your dedication to the projects you create.

As a developer I understand the idea of teamwork and enjoy working with others. But as my personality goes, sometimes I do my best work alone. This requires a lot of self-management and the ability to keep myself motivated. I don’t believe on leaving things in the middle, I either get there or die trying.

As any person I’m not perfect and it is always my goal to grow in a positive way as a professional as well as a human being. Self-constructive criticism is one of the things I always see as completely necessary for a healthy work environment and personal growth.

Last but not least...at all, as a hardcore gamer I’ve always found a deep interest in video games. I’ve admire gaming companies my whole life not only as the best set of developers/designers/animators/administrative teams, because I really don’t think there is a more complex piece of art than a game, but also as the most caring and creative group of people you can be a part of, and I dream of being a part of the joy brought to millions of players from a world they could never even imagine if it wasn’t for the people behind these amazing projects. My dream is to be a part of the adventure these companies take you on.
