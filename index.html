About Me

I’m Victor Herrera, a software engineer and web developer with a love for simplistic yet amazing web solutions and the world of video game development. Though I could just describe to you my experience and knowledge I honestly think that’s what my LinkedIn Profile is for, which you are more than welcome to check out. Instead I’d rather describe myself as a person and a little bit of what it means to be me as a professional.

Over the years I have found myself caring to learn about the visual concept of my projects, some people have the wrong idea that as developers we are a bit “square”, but I think we are creative beings beyond the uderstanding of so many, and we can still be the logical geniuses they see us as. I believe this way of seeing yourself as a developer makes an interesting combination towards delivering only the best solutions and giving all your care to the project you are creating.

As a developer I understand the idea of teamwork and embrace it completely. But as my personality goes, sometimes I do my best work alone to a certain point to then integrate it with the group. This requires a lot of self-management and the ability to keep myself motivated. I don’t believe on leaving things in the middle, I either get there or die trying.

As any person I’m not perfect and it is always my goal to grow in a positive way as a professional as well as a human being. Self-constructive criticism is one of the things I always see as completely necessary for a healthy work environment and personal growth.

Last but not least...at all, as a hardcore gamer I’ve always found a deep interest in being part of a game development company and even expand my abilities from web development to game development along the way. I’ve admire gaming companies my whole life not only as the best set of developers/designers/animators/administrative teams, because I really don’t think there is a more complex piece of art than a game, but also as the most caring and creative group of people you can be a part of, and I dream of being a part of the joy brought to millions of players from a world they could never even imagine if it wasn’t for the people behind these amazing projects. My dream is to be a part of the adventure these companies take you on.
