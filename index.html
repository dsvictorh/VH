About Me

My name is Víctor...duh. No but really, nice to meet you! I am a software engineer who got blown away by the world of web development. Sometimes people think of us programmers as “square” people who create logical solutions. Although this is true and I do have my own part of that I never quite felt I fitted under that description, because in my opinion I was never a programmer, I am a developer... What do I mean by this? What I think the difference between those two is that the programmer is given a specific job, most likely thought of and THEN he or she spits the code. They are really good at it, their code is amazing and their solutions based on optimized code are awesome as well, but they never seem to ask my favorite question: Why?

Why are they asking me to do this? Why do they think this is what they want? Why are they not looking at this instead of that? Why do they want it designed that way? See... Usually a programmer concentrates on the how and that’s more than Ok because that’ what we need to do in the end, provide the how to the solution of an identified problem. But we often forget what technology is and to me it is the combination of passion and dedication of a group of people determined to make the world better, funnier and most of all more interesting. We tend to forget why is it that we do what we do, and maybe this is just me but I don’t code for mysel,f I code for others, even my personal projects I meticulously check them for quality so it is easy on the eyes for other developers.
